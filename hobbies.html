Fishing

Fishing is one of my favorite outdoor activities, enjoyed year-round through both summer and ice fishing. I often fish with family and friends, targeting species such as Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, and Perch. I recently took a trip up north just south of the boundary waters in Lutsen and went out and caught a whole bunch of walleye. In the picture to the left I am holding up the biggest northern I have every caught, measuring 37.5 inches. This northern was really speical for me because I caught it while in a kayak. It was a really rewarding fight because it took so long to try and land it while being in a kayak.
